Family Law Services

Our Telluride family law attorneys handle all aspects of domestic relations, including:

  • Divorce and Legal Separation: Including high-asset divorces involving real estate, business interests, and second homes
  • Child Custody and Parenting Time: Crafting parenting plans that work with Telluride's unique lifestyle and seasonal considerations
  • Child Support: Navigating Colorado's child support guidelines with attention to executive compensation and variable income
  • Spousal Support: Determining appropriate maintenance in high-income cases
  • Property Division: Handling complex asset division including luxury properties, ski-in/ski-out real estate, and business valuations
  • Prenuptial and Postnuptial Agreements: Protecting assets before and during marriage

Criminal Defense

  • DUI/DWAI: Defending against drunk and impaired driving charges, including cases involving tourists and visitors
  • Domestic Violence: Providing vigorous defense and guidance through protection order proceedings
  • Drug Offenses: Handling possession, distribution, and marijuana-related charges
  • Assault and Theft: Defending against violent crimes and property offenses
  • Traffic Violations: Fighting speeding tickets, reckless driving, and other traffic offenses
  • Municipal Violations: Representing clients in Telluride Municipal Court
